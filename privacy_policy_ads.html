Privacy Policy: Ads and Ad Consent

Effective date: April 1, 2026

This notice explains how Stimmapp handles advertising, ad-related data, and ad consent choices.

When ads are shown

Ads may be shown to users of the free version of the app. Pro users do not receive ads while Pro is active.

What data may be processed

Advertising providers may process device identifiers, app activity, approximate location derived from IP address, technical device information, and ad-performance data. This depends on platform rules, provider requirements, and your consent choice where consent is legally required.

Consent in the EEA, UK, and Switzerland

If you are in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, Stimmapp asks for consent before personalized advertising is enabled where required by law. If you do not grant consent, ads stay disabled until you make a choice.

How your choice is stored

We store your ad-consent choice in your account settings together with the time the choice was updated. This lets the app apply your preference across sessions and devices where your account is used.

Changing your choice

You can change or withdraw your ad-consent choice at any time in the app's Privacy Settings.

Service providers

Ads may be delivered through AdMob or related Google advertising services. Their privacy information is available here: AdMob Privacy.

Contact

Questions about ads or ad-consent processing can be sent to support@trainvent.com.
